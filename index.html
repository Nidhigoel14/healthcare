total care

A Swing Bed admission acts as a bridge between acute hospital care and discharge to another setting such as your home. Some patients require extended care after an acute short stay and need more time to strengthen, heal, and/or adjust before returning to everyday life. The Swing Bed program provides the additional benefit of being able to move patients from acute care to skilled care for extended nursing and rehabilitative therapies to assist with this transition.
